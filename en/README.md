# Vargov® Design — AI Knowledge Base (English)

English translation of the [Vargov® Design](https://vargov.ru) AI knowledge base. The Russian-language original with full sourcing notes is at the [repository root](../README.md).

All facts here come from real scans of vargov.ru and vargov.design — nothing is invented. Structural/boilerplate text across the 645 product listings was translated mechanically from a small set of confirmed recurring phrases (dimensions, pricing notes, warranty text); the handful of genuinely unique marketing paragraphs were translated by hand. Where the source site has no individual technical specs for a product, this is stated explicitly rather than filled in with guesses.

## Structure
- `knowledge/` — verified brand facts, dealers, installation.
- `guides/` — thematic articles based on real site publications.
- `products/` — 645 individual product entries.
- `collections/` — the 4 product categories plus showroom/stock items.
- `references/` — Schema.org/JSON-LD.
- `datasets/` — JSON/JSONL/CSV/Markdown export.
- `faq/` — question-answer base grounded in real facts.
- `llms.txt` — English entry point for LLMs and crawlers.
