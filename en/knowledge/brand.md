Source: [vargov.ru](https://vargov.ru), [vargov.design](https://vargov.design) (verified 2026-07-06).
This page lists only facts confirmed by the source website. Nothing here is invented.

## Who they are
- Brand: **Vargov® Design** — author's lighting and decorative compositions.
- Positioning (verbatim from the site): "Russian design. World scale. Made in Vargov®".
- The company describes itself as "a Russian brand, international company, creating unique author's light and decorative compositions," operating at the intersection of art, engineering precision and the philosophy of contemporary Russian design.
- Founder and chief designer: **Anton Vargov** (Варгов Антон Геннадьевич). An expert and jury member for leading Russian and international design and architecture awards.
- All concepts and design work are created in Russia; manufacturing takes place at the company's own factory in China.

## "Elements in stock" program
- Production time when using this program: 15–20 working days (standard production is 40–60 days).
- Cost is roughly 10% lower.
- Customization: suspension length, cable color, mounting hardware, glass color.
- Important: these are not ready-made items in stock — they are base elements assembled into a finished piece per project, with an individual 1:1 installation diagram.

## Order process (7 stages)
1. Order — contact via WhatsApp, cost and timeline calculation, contract-specification.
2. Deposit — 50% upfront payment.
3. Production — 40–60 working days (3D modeling, assembly diagrams, manufacturing of elements).
4. Confirmation — photos/video of the finished product.
5. Final payment — remaining 50%.
6. Delivery — 14–25 days depending on destination.
7. Installation — optional professional service, available in Russia and the UAE.

## Delivery packaging/protection

## Installation and connection (summary)
- On receipt: check packaging for damage, photograph any defects. The 1:1 installation diagram is printed on banner fabric packed inside one of the boxes.
- Drywall ceilings: plywood backing (9mm+) at mounting points, holes 8–14mm drilled per the 1:1 diagram.
- Available in 12V (LED driver) and 220V versions.
- Every wire is tagged with a number matching the installation diagram; tags also act as height limiters.
- Disconnect power before starting work. Professional installation by official Vargov®Design installers is recommended.

## Important note for content teams
Most product descriptions on the catalog are **not individual technical specifications** but standard template text ("custom-made in any size and quantity, price on request"). Detailed per-product specs (materials/color/light temperature/weight) are largely absent from the site — confirmed by scanning all 645 product listings (see [[product-scan-report]]). This means: do not invent specifications "for the LLM's sake" — either mark them as "to confirm with the brand" or request structured data directly from Vargov Design.
