# Lighting vs. decorative composition — what's the difference (per Vargov® Design)

Source: [vargov.ru/tpost/je6ofjylh1-svetovaya-ili-dekorativnaya-kompozitsiya](https://vargov.ru/tpost/je6ofjylh1-svetovaya-ili-dekorativnaya-kompozitsiya)

## Lighting composition
The primary function is illumination — main, accent, or decorative lighting. The focus is on choosing light sources, their placement and light control to achieve a specific visual effect (LED panels, fixtures, light strips, target brightness levels).

## Decorative composition
The priority is aesthetics and the visual enrichment of an interior: the design, shape, color and texture of the elements, rather than functional lighting. These can include static or dynamic elements (sculptures, panels, textiles) that may be illuminated but serve primarily as visual ornamentation.

## Overlap between categories
Vargov Design explicitly acknowledges that these categories can overlap: lighting elements often perform a decorative function, merging illumination and design into a single piece.

This distinction is the basis for how the site's catalog is organized (see [[brand]], the "Lighting compositions" and "Decorative compositions" sections).
