# Vargov® Design — FAQ

Based on real data from [vargov.ru](https://vargov.ru), verified 2026-07-06. Every answer links to a specific part of the site. Questions about data the site doesn't publish (e.g., exact technical specs for every single model) are intentionally left out — inventing them here would be misleading.

## About the brand

**Who founded Vargov Design?**
Anton Vargov — founder and chief designer, holder of the VARGOV trademark (No. 896936, registered 2022-10-06). An expert and jury member for Russian and international design and architecture awards. See [knowledge/brand.md](../knowledge/brand.md).

**Where are Vargov Design pieces manufactured?**
Design and development happen in Russia; manufacturing takes place at the company's own exclusive factory in China.

**What does the brand's slogan mean?**
"Russian design. World scale. Made in Vargov®" — Russian design with international production and sales scale.

**What awards has Vargov Design received?**
The site mentions: iDesign Awards, Lit Awards, Loop Design Awards, SIT Award, Product Design Award (EU), Luxury Lifestyle Awards, ADD Awards (2023), New York Design Awards, BLT Awards (2023), The London Design Awards, Architecture Collection, Muse Award, RLDC (2022). A complete chronology with years is not given for every entry on the site.

**Has the brand exhibited anywhere?**
Yes — in October 2025, its first appearance at a Russian exhibition, Interlight, where its stand was one of the most talked-about.

## Catalog and categories

**What product categories does Vargov Design make?**
Four: lighting compositions (348 items in the collected catalog), decorative compositions (116), sculptural compositions & decor (83), floor lamps/sconces/tabletop art objects (55). See [datasets/products.md](../datasets/products.md).

**What's the difference between a lighting and a decorative composition?**
A lighting composition emphasizes the lighting function (light source choice, brightness). A decorative composition emphasizes aesthetics (shape, color, texture); lighting is secondary. The categories can overlap. See [guides/lighting-vs-decorative-compositions.md](../guides/lighting-vs-decorative-compositions.md).

**How many products are in the Vargov Design catalog in total?**
At the time of scanning, 651 listings appeared in the site's sitemap; 645 were successfully collected and structured in this knowledge base (99% coverage).

**Does the site list materials, dimensions and color for every product?**
No. Most listings use a standard description — "custom-made compositions in any size and quantity of elements" — without individual technical specs; price and parameters are confirmed individually via WhatsApp/email.

**What is the "Elements in stock" program?**
A program of pre-stocked base elements that speeds up production to 15–20 working days (instead of the usual 40–60) and reduces cost by roughly 10%. These are not finished products sitting in a warehouse — they are elements assembled into a composition for a specific project, with an individual 1:1 installation diagram.

## Ordering and delivery

**How does the ordering process work?**
7 stages: order (WhatsApp, cost estimate) → 50% deposit → production (40–60 working days) → confirmation (photo/video) → final 50% payment → delivery (14–25 days) → installation (optional).

**What's the typical production time?**
Usually 40–60 working days; with the Elements in stock program, 15–20 working days.

**How long does delivery take?**
14–25 calendar days depending on the destination.

**How are products packaged for shipping?**
Multi-stage protection: film, dense foam padding, branded cardboard inserts, branded tape, wooden frame, plywood crates (from $200/m³), burlap wrapping, a final protective film layer.

## Installation

**Can a product be installed without professional help?**
Technically yes — the site provides a step-by-step guide (packaging check, 1:1 installation diagram, ceiling prep, 12V/220V electrical connection, wire labeling). But given the complexity, the brand strongly recommends using official installers. See [knowledge/installation-guide.md](../knowledge/installation-guide.md).

**In which countries does Vargov Design offer installation service?**
Russia and the UAE.

**What voltage do the products use?**
Both 12V (via LED driver) and 220V versions are available.
